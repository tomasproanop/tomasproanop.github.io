Teaching Experience

With over 5 years of experience as a university lecturer at Universidad de las Artes in Guayaquil, Ecuador, I have taught a variety of courses in music performance, harmony, ear training and theory. My curriculum integrates traditional music studies with modern technology—covering digital portfolio creation (including recording and mixing), music scoring using dedicated software, and efficient management of digital scores for gigs and future arrangements. My teaching approach emphasizes creativity, technical skill development, and a deep understanding of music as both an art and a discipline.

I also incorporate the use of free and open-source software (FOSS) to help my students develop their musical ears and transcription skills. This practical approach, combined with my experience working with students from diverse backgrounds, ensures that whether you’re a beginner or an advanced musician, you gain the tools you need to succeed in both performance and digital music management.

If you are interested in lessons or workshops—whether to enhance your performance, build your digital portfolio, or master music transcription—please feel free to contact me. I look forward to sharing my passion for music and education with you.
